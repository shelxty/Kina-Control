# Kina Control

Papua New Guinea's economy is in need of your help! Operate their economy, controlling government spending, taxation rate, and interest on reserves rate to influence Papua New Guinea's GDP, inflation rate, and more! 

Make sure your public discontent levels don't go above 90%, or you'll lose -- your citizens will riot. Use the dials to improve your economy, but don't make a mistake!

Play at https://shelx.itch.io/kina-control
